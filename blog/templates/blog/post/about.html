About Us


Our Mission

Kenya Climate Youth Corps is dedicated to empowering the youth to take action against climate change. Our mission is to educate, inspire, and mobilize young people around the world to lead the charge in creating a sustainable future.


Our Vision

We envision a world where the next generation is fully equipped and motivated to address climate challenges. Through collaborative efforts, we aim to foster innovative solutions that mitigate the impacts of climate change and promote environmental justice.


Our Values

  • Sustainability: Committed to promoting practices that ensure the long-term health of our planet.
  • Education: Providing resources and opportunities for learning about climate issues.
  • Empowerment: Enabling young people to become leaders in their communities.
  • Collaboration: Working together with individuals, organizsations and governments to drive change.
  • Discovery: Seeking new ideas and ways to deal with climate issues
  • Innovation: Actively coming up with science technological advanced ideas to curb climate change.

Our team

Our team comprised of passionate individuals from diverse backgrounds, all united by a common goal: to make a positive impact on our planet. From environmental scientists to community organizers, each member brings unique skills and perspectives to the table.
